Filter

You can focus on any pairs of sounds or work with all sounds by selecting the sounds of interest using the filter option (often depending on your first language and current English language proficiency).

To adjust your filter:

  1. Tap from Home, Learn or Instructions page (first page) of Practice and Quizzes.
  2. Select a sound from the left column.
  3. Select a corresponding sound from the right column. (These are two contrasting sounds.)
  4. Your selected sounds appear at the top of the page (e.g., Learn p vs. b or All vs. All)
