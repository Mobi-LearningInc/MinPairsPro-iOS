Sound chart

An interactive phonemic chart comprising a list of sounds with their phonetic symbols. All symbols/sounds have example words illustrated with images. It allows you to listen to an individual sound in isolation as well as to hear the sound in a word. MinimalPairs offers also offers you a visual stimuli and comprehension support by showing illustrations for the example words. It includes all the sounds of English vowels, consonants and double-vowels (diphthongs).

To use sound chart:

  1. Tap a sound to hear it once.
  2. Tap the sound again to hear and see an example word.
  3. Tap the sound again to hear it one more time.
  4. Tap the sound repeatedly (again and again) to hear and see more examples.
