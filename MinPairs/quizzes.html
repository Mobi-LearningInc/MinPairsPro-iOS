Quizzes

Very similar to practice but your questions are timed (see the timer on the Submit button). You can assess your newly acquired English pronunciation skills. At the end of each attempt (10 questions), you have a chance to review your answers (tap Details) and to share the results through social media (tap Share). Different quiz modes are provided integrating listening, reading and writing skills. The timing of the practice can be customized in Settings.

To use quizzes:

  1. Make sure the filter is adjusted to your preferred settings (Are these the sounds you want to listen to?) Click to select the sounds you want to learn and follow the steps for Filter (above).
  2. There are three types of activities:
    • Listen to the sound > Select from the two images the one matching the sound (the one you hear) > Submit your answer
    • Listen to the word > Type the word you heard > Submit your answer
    • Read the word on top > Listen to both words > Select the matching sound > Submit your answer
      Note: You can quit any time by tapping the red X in the top left corner.
  3. Your practice results are summarized: how many questions you answered correctly and how much time you took.
  4. Tap Details to review the questions you got wrong, or Share to share your results using social media, or Continue to return to Home where you can select any option form the main menu.
